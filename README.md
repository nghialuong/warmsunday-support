## FAQ
**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your reflections and weekly data stay on your device. WarmSunday does not collect or upload personal data.

**What does WarmSunday Pro include?**
Share cards, deeper history and more — via a simple annual plan or a one-time Founder Pass.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app.

**Languages?**
Fully bilingual: English and Vietnamese, switchable in Settings.

**How do I delete my data?**
Settings → Delete Data inside the app, or delete the app.
